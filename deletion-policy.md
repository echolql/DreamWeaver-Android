Account & Data Deletion Request

How to Delete Your DreamWeaver account:

We value your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated personal information, please follow the steps below.

How to request deletion:
Option 1 (In-App): Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.

Option 2 (Email): If you can no longer access the app, please email us at echolql@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request." Please include the email address associated with your account.

What happens when you delete your account?
Personal Data: Your profile information, including your username, email, and preferences, will be permanently removed from our active servers.

Associated Data: Any data collected during your use of the app (e.g., activity logs) will be deleted.

Retention Period: Once requested, your account will be deactivated immediately. Complete data removal from our backups may take up to 7 days to ensure full system synchronization.

Exceptions: We may retain certain information if required by law or for legitimate security purposes (such as fraud prevention), as outlined in our Privacy Policy: https://github.com/echolql/DreamWeaver-Android/blob/main/Privacy-Policy.md
